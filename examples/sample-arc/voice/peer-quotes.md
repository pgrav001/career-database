## How this file is used

The artifact-generation flow pulls from this file in a specific order:

1. **Resume:** quote rarely. When a quote is used, it goes in the Summary or as a bullet under the role, attributed by source ("system director" not "Pat") unless the source is independently famous.
2. **LinkedIn About:** one quote, maximum. Pick the strongest external-attested one (Jorge's, in this case — the RLC newsletter is public).
3. **Cover letter:** lead with a quote when the role audience is one this quote will resonate with. Jorge's is good for cross-sector roles; Pat's is good for library-system roles.
4. **Interview prep:** all of them, organized by which strength they back.
