## Pattern-match for inbound recruiter outreach

When a recruiter pings you, this is the quick rubric for whether to engage:

- ✅ Lane + level match
- ✅ Geography match
- ✅ No hard nos triggered
- ✅ Company / role is at least somewhat aligned with the "best fit" or "acceptable" descriptions above

If 3 of 4, engage. If <3, polite decline with a sentence on what you're actually looking for.
