## Voice rules of thumb

1. **Lead with the specific, follow with the abstract.** Number first, frame second.
2. **Honest about constraints + costs.** Don't hide the trade-off.
3. **Conversational asides are fine** in self-review register; less in resume register.
4. **Past tense for delivered work, future-conditional for plans.** "I built X" / "We'd want to do Y."
5. **Avoid the corporate hero voice.** No "spearheaded" / "transformational impact." Use "led", "drove", "designed", "built."
6. **Lessons + what-I-learned at the end of a story.** Most distinguishing feature of mature writers.
