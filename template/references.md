# References

> _Who would vouch for you, and what they'd say._

## Conventions

- **Tier 1:** ask anytime, will pick up the phone, will speak to anything
- **Tier 2:** ask with warning, can speak to a specific scope
- **Tier 3:** would respond positively to a written check / LinkedIn message
